Why this exists

Everyone's talking about quantum computing, billion-parameter models, and the future of intelligence. That's important work. But right now, today, a freelancer in Karachi is building an e-commerce site for a local business. A startup in Istanbul is launching a food delivery app. A solo developer in São Paulo is trying to figure out if their product is missing something obvious.

They don't need AGI. They need a checklist.

Not a blog post with opinions. Not a consultant charging $10,000. A real, data-backed answer to a simple question: "Does my app have what users actually expect?"

So we built it. We pointed a scraper at the top 30 websites in 19 countries. We used AI to analyze what features they all share. We counted. If 90% of top e-commerce sites have a search bar — that's not an opinion, that's a standard. If 40% have a wishlist — that's a nice-to-have, not a must.

The data is open. The methodology is transparent. The benchmarks belong to everyone.

How it works
01
We visit the top 30 websites in each country
An automated scraper powered by Playwright visits each website, extracts the full page structure — navigation, buttons, forms, images, accessibility attributes — and takes screenshots at desktop and mobile viewports.
02
AI analyzes what each site has
GPT-4o examines the extracted structure and identifies which of 45 tracked features are present — from product search and shopping cart to cookie consent and alt text on images.
03
We count frequencies, not opinions
If 18 out of 20 sites have order tracking, that's 90% — classified as CRITICAL. If 8 out of 20 have a wishlist, that's 40% — classified as RECOMMENDED. The numbers decide, not us.
04
You check your app against the benchmark
Send your features to our API. Get back a score, a list of what's missing, and how you compare to the top players in your industry and country. In your CI/CD pipeline, on every pull request, automatically.
The API
// Check your app against Pakistan's e-commerce benchmark POST /check { "industry": "ecommerce_pakistan", "features": ["product_search", "shopping_cart", "login_signup"] } // Response { "score": 25, "verdict": "INCOMPLETE", "critical_missing": [ "category_browsing", "return_refund_info", "help_center", "alt_text_on_images", ... 7 more ] }
