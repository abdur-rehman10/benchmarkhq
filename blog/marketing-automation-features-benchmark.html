Marketing Automation: 29 Critical Features (The Most in SaaS)

March 31, 2026 · 4 min read

Marketing automation tools have 29 critical features — the highest count of any SaaS category in our dataset. The market is mature, competitive, and deeply standardized.

Why So Many Critical Features

Marketing automation is inherently complex. Tools must handle email campaigns, landing pages, lead capture forms, lead scoring, CRM integration, analytics, A/B testing, audience segmentation, workflow automation, and multi-channel reporting. Each capability is individually critical because the category exists to do all of them simultaneously.

The Critical Feature List

These features appear on 80%+ of marketing automation websites: visual workflow builder, email template editor, lead scoring, CRM sync, landing page builder, form builder, analytics dashboard, A/B testing, segmentation, drip campaigns, and integration marketplace.

The Contrast

Compare this to AI/ML platforms (only 2 critical features) or scheduling tools (7 critical features). Marketing automation's high count reflects decades of market maturity. Newer categories haven't had time to converge on standards yet.
