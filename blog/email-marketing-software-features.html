Email Marketing Software: 17 Features That Define the Category

March 31, 2026 · 4 min read

Email marketing is one of the most standardized SaaS categories with 17 critical features. The market is mature, and user expectations are well-established.

The Critical Features

These appear on 80%+ of email marketing tool websites:

  • Drag-and-drop email builder
  • Subscriber list management
  • Basic automation sequences (drip campaigns)
  • Analytics (opens, clicks, bounces, unsubscribes)
  • Template library
  • Segmentation by attributes
  • A/B subject line testing
  • Signup form builder
  • Integration marketplace

The Differentiators

Features in the 30-60% range (RECOMMENDED) are where tools differentiate: predictive send-time optimization, landing page builders, deliverability monitoring dashboards, advanced behavioral segmentation, and AI content suggestions.
